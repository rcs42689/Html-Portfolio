The Best Movies According to Me

My Top 3 Movies with Justin Long

Zach And Miri Make A Porno

This film directed by Kevin Smith has a wonderful array of characters including a fantastic display by Justin Long playing a Porn actor whose stoic nature brings tears of laughter with his witty comments and general mannerisms.

Tusk

Another Kevin Smith film where Justin long shows his ability to create characters with wit and general comedic mannerisms. A horror film by genre it also has a definite comedic value that permeates Kevin Smith films.

Waiting

Starring a cast of comedians this film delves into the stressful, drama filled restaurant business and showcases that being servers can trauma bond any group of employees.
